David Chukwuemeka Oladapo Edwards is a multidisciplinary creative and the visionary force behind Eddy’s Collexion — a design brand driven by purpose, culture, and originality. As Creative Director, David leads with an instinct for visual storytelling that transcends trends, merging bold aesthetics with thoughtful communication. With a background rooted in graphic design, branding, and visual identity, David's work reflects a deep commitment to intentionality and artistic freedom. Eddy’s Collexion is not just a studio, it’s a movement that challenges norms and creates space for expression, especially within the African creative landscape.

David’s portfolio spans digital campaigns, editorial design, brand development, and experimental visuals with each project crafted with a balance of strategy and soul. Through every creation, he seeks to build meaningful connections between art, people, and impact.
